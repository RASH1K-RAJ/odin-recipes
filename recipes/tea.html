Tea

Description

Tea is another household name in India. An average Indian neraly consumes 3 cups of tes every day. There is no better way of starting a day than taking tea and readin a newspaper. Tea is often served to guests in Indian households. Tea creates bonds between people.

Ingredients

  • tea leaves
  • water
  • milk
  • sugar

Steps

  1. Take half a cup of water and warm it up
  2. Put a tea spoon of tea leaves in, and leave it for 2 mins
  3. Pour 1 cup of milk
  4. Put 1/2 tea spoon of sugar
  5. Boil it for 5 more minutes and your tea is ready
