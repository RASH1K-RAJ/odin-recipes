Boiled Eggs

Description

Boiled Eggs are one of the best source of proteins. From child to bodybuilders everyone needs proteins and what better source of protein than boiled eggs. During winters these are one of the bets snacks to have with a pinch of salt and some onions.

Ingridients

  • Eggs
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Take a pan of water and warm it up
  2. Put a few Eggs inside the water
  3. Let it boil for 20 minutes or so
  4. Take out the eggs and peel the shell
  5. Cut the eggs in half and serve it with sald and onions
